Common Questions

I'm already on Facebook. Do I really need a website?

Social media is great for engagement, but you don't own it. A website gives you complete control and shows up in Google searches where 70% of customers start their buying journey. Plus, it looks more professional and trustworthy.

How long does it take?

Most websites are completed in 2 weeks from our initial consultation. Premium packages with priority service can be ready in as little as 1 week.

What if I need changes later?

All packages include 30 days of free support. After that, you can update it yourself (we'll train you) or opt for our maintenance plan starting at $79/month.

Do you provide hosting?

Yes, all packages include professional hosting for the first year. After that, it's just $15/month. We handle all technical aspects including security and updates.

Can I update the website myself?

Absolutely. We build on easy-to-use platforms and provide full training. No coding required. Or if you prefer, we can handle all updates through our maintenance service.
